Net interest income rose 2.04%, but net fee and commission increased fell 22.16%, year on year, by the end of the fourth quarter.

Mega’s customer deposits increased by 12.12% to NPR 153 billion, and loans and advances to BFIs and retailers climbed by just 7.5 % up to the fourth quarter.
